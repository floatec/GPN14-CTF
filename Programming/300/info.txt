Programming 300 - Superbrain
=================================

Description
------------
Superbrain: "This server wants to play with us. Try to get the flag"


Steps
-------
Play mastermind. The first five rounds have no timelimit, afterwars you have to answer within 1 second.
You need to guess the right combination which are 4 letter combinations of 0-9.
X marks right letter on right position, O marks right letter on wrong position and - marks invalid number.
